Overview

As a college student, have you had these problems?

Have you ever struggled with deadlines? Have you ever procrastinated? Have you ever had trouble balancing social life and school life? Academic research has shown that higher-achievers in college are more goal-motivated and able to better organize study. Jarvis aims to help college students to become more successful.

Jarvis is here to help

Jarvis aims to solve such problems. Jarvis enables the you to SET academic goals, TRACK study behaviors, and SYNC academic performance from academic accounts. Based on past performance and current goals, Jarvis can generate a personalized STUDY PLAN.
